# Aura AI: Global HR Policy (v1.0)

## 1. Leave Policy
- Casual Leave: 12 days per year.
- Sick Leave: 10 days per year.
- Maternity Leave: 26 weeks.
- Paternity Leave: 2 weeks.

## 2. Working Hours
- Standard hours: 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
- Flexible start time allowed between 8:00 AM and 10:00 AM.
- Remote work: Up to 2 days per week with manager approval.

## 3. Reimbursements
- Internet allowance: $50 per month.
- Learning & Development: $1000 per year for certifications.
- Travel: All official travel must be booked via the Aura AI portal.

## 4. Performance
- Reviews are conducted bi-annually in June and December.
- Promotions are based on "Neural Score" (Productivity + Feedback).
